# Store Graphics Brief — Numjoy: Number Puzzle Games

For the graphics team. This describes the **screens** and **colors** of the app
so you can design the Play Store / App Store screenshots and feature graphic.
Everything below is exactly what the real screens show.

---

## Brand colors (hex)

**Main background gradient (Aurora — the default look):**
`#5B3DF5` → `#A23DF5` → `#F53D9E` (purple to pink, top-left to bottom-right)

**Accents / UI:**
| Color | Hex |
|---|---|
| Coin gold | `#F5C542` |
| Title gold (accent word) | `#FFC93C` |
| Streak flame / warm | `#FF8A3D` |
| Brand pink (buttons, active) | `#FF5C8A` |
| Success green | `#34C759` |
| Wrong / warning orange | `#FF8A5C` |
| Purple (buttons on white) | `#6A2DBF` |
| Text on white cards | `#241139` |
| White cards | `#FFFFFF` |

**Per-game accent:** 2048 = `#FFC12E` · Number Tap = `#4FC3F7` · Number Sort =
`#7BE86B` · Magic Square = `#B14CFF` · Daily = `#FF6FA5`

**Number tile colors** (numbers always use these): 2 `#D9DBF7` · 4 `#7FE0D2` ·
8 `#FFB23F` · 16 `#FF5B5B` · 32 `#FF4D9D` · 64 `#FF6A3D` · 128 `#FFC93C` ·
256 `#54CC57` · 512 `#3FA3F0` · 1024 `#B14CFF` · 2048 `#FFC12E`

**Other themes (background gradients), if you want variety in screenshots:**
- Midnight Neon: `#1C1140` → `#0C0D18`
- Ocean Breeze: `#0277BD` → `#26C6DA` → `#80DEEA`
- Sunset Glow: `#2B1055` → `#7D2A6B` → `#FF8A5C`
- Emerald Forest: `#0F3D2E` → `#1D6B4F` → `#5FC28C`
- Royal Gold: `#2A2417` → `#0D0D0D` (gold accent `#D4AF37`)
- Candy Pop: `#A18CD1` → `#C197E8` → `#FBC2EB`

> All screens sit on the gradient background. Cards are white or frosted
> (semi-transparent white). Text on the gradient is white.

## Screens & their content

### 1. Home
- Big heading: **"Number Games"** (bold, white)
- Subtitle: **"Play, solve, and train your brain!"**
- Two white stat pills: 🔥 **"5 day"** / "Streak" · 🪙 **"240"** / "Coins"
- Top-right: a palette/theme button 🎨 and a gift button 🎁 (with a small red dot when a reward is ready)
- Row heading: **"Games"** with a **"See all ›"** link
- Featured white card — **2048** · "Classic merge puzzle" · "Best: 12480 👑" · purple-pink **"Play Now ▶"** button · small 4×4 board preview with colored tiles
- Compact card — **Daily Challenge**
- Bottom nav bar with two tabs: **Home** | **All Games**

### 2. All Games
- Header: **"All Games"** + a 🔥 streak pill
- Hero card (pink gradient `#FF7EB3`→`#C84BD6`): badge **"⭐ DAILY CHALLENGE"** · **"Daily Challenge"** · "Reach 512 in fewest moves" · white **"Play Now"** button
- Frosted list cards, each: colored icon chip + title + subtitle + "👑 Best …":
  - **2048** — Classic merge puzzle
  - **Number Tap** — Tap 1–25, beat the clock
  - **Number Sort** — Sort the columns in fewest moves
  - **Magic Square** — Make every line sum to 15

### 3. Daily Reward
- Header: **"Daily Reward"** + coin balance pill
- Featured warm-gradient card: badge **"⭐ DAY 5 · READY"** · big 🎁 · **"+60"** with coin icon · "5-day streak — keep it going!"
- Label **"This week"** + a horizontal strip of 7 days:
  - D1=10, D2=20, D3=30, D4=40, D5=60, D6=80, **D7=150 🏆**
  - Past days show a green ✓, today is highlighted white, Day 7 is gold
- White button: **"Claim +60 Reward"**

### 4. 2048 (game)
- Score header: **Score** and **Best** boxes
- Line: "Join the tiles, get to **2048!**" + small palette / sound / help / **New Game** buttons
- 4×4 board of colored number tiles (2, 4, 8, 16, …)
- **Undo** button (lock icon for free users)
- Hint text: "Swipe ← ↑ → ↓ to move"
- Win card: "You reached 2048! 🎉"

### 5. Number Tap (game)
- Title: **NUMBER TAP** (the word "TAP" in gold)
- Two stat cards: 👑 **Best Time** · ⏱ **Time** (live)
- A 5×5 grid of numbers **1–25**; the next number to tap is highlighted
- Hint: "Tap numbers in order"
- Win card: "Great job! 🎉" / "New record! 🎉" with your time

### 6. Number Sort (game)
- Title: **NUMBER SORT** (the word "SORT" in gold)
- Two stat cards: 👑 **Best** (moves) · ⇅ **Moves**
- Board: columns of stacked colored number tokens (drag the top one onto a matching one or an empty column)
- Two pill buttons: **Undo** (shows remaining, e.g. "Undo · 3") · **Restart**
- Hint: "Drag a number onto a matching one or an empty column."
- Win card: "Solved it! 🎉" with the move count

### 7. Magic Square (game)
- Title: **MAGIC SQUARE** (the word "SQUARE" in gold)
- Two stat cards: 👑 **Best Time** · ⏱ **Time**
- A 3×3 grid on a dark frame; **column sums along the top**, **row sums down the left**, **"=15"** in the corner, diagonals **↘ ↙** below
- Sums turn **green** when a line sums to 15
- A tray of number tiles to drag in (locked clue tiles show a 🔒)
- Two pill buttons: **Hint** · **New**
- Win card: "Magic! 🎉" with your time

### 8. Themes
- A theme picker showing all 7 looks (Aurora free; 6 Premium). Great for a
  "7 beautiful themes" screenshot — show the same game in 2–3 different theme colors.
