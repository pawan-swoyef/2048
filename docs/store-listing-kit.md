## 3. What's in the app (content)

### The five games
| Game | One-liner | Scored by |
|---|---|---|
| **2048** | Slide & merge tiles to reach 2048 | High score |
| **Number Tap** | Tap 1 → 25 as fast as you can | Best time |
| **Number Sort** | Sort the columns into single colors in the fewest moves | Fewest moves |
| **Magic Square** | Drag 1–9 so every row, column & diagonal sums to 15 | Best time |
| **Daily Challenge** | A fresh puzzle every day — reach 512 in the fewest moves | New daily |

### Engagement & rewards
- **Daily streak** 🔥 — keep playing day after day.
- **Coins** 🪙 — earned from the daily gift and milestones.
- **Daily Reward** — a 7-day cycle paying **10 · 20 · 30 · 40 · 60 · 80 · 150** coins, with **milestone bonuses** at 3, 7, 14 and 30 days (+30, +70, +150, +500).
- **7 themes** — Aurora free; six more unlock with Premium.
- **Auto-save** — every best score is kept locally.
- **Share** your results as an image.

### Premium
Ad-free experience · unlimited Undo · unlimited Hints · all 6 bonus themes unlocked.

### Monetization & data (store declarations)
- Ads: **banner + interstitial + rewarded** (rewarded video powers free Undo/Hint). Declare *Contains ads = Yes*.
- In-app purchases: **Premium** (subscription / one-time).
- Plays offline; scores stored on-device.
- **Privacy Policy URL is required** (app shows ads) — see [`privacy-policy.html`](privacy-policy.html). Host it and add the URL before publishing.
